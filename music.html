Get Started

  • Recording

    At Global Fussion, we specialize in recording and mixing. Whether youre tracking a full band, recording some live piano or cutting a pop/hip-hop vocal, weve got everything you need. Our A Studio is our primary tracking room for drums and piano recordings, while both Studio A and Studio B are fully equipped for tracking overdubs and vocals. We have mic preamps available from Neve, SSL, Tube Tech, and more.

  • Mixing

    We have a long history of mixing major and independent records! Whether youre in the UK or sending work directly, we can work with you to achieve that perfect mix. Our Studio is home to our SSL Duality 48 channel console, allowing for tons of routing flexibility, while offering classic features such as the Quad compressor and E series EQ. The Duality, combined with ProTools HDX and a host of classic outboard gear, make for a powerful mix environment.

  • Production

    For a custom production, the role of producer varies based on many factors, and our production team can provide any or all of the services as required. Whether you are looking to put out a single, make an EP or create a full length record, Global Fussion will guide you through the process and make the end result something that stands out, is commercially competitive, and is a completely custom production for you.

  • Mastering

    Global Fussion has proved high quality mastering services for both independent and major label clients for almost 20 years. We have had the privilege of working on some amazing records over the years. Tony Kehn, who joined the team after the closing of the legendary Hit Factory in 2005, handles each project personally to maintain the highest professional standards.

  • ADR/Voice Over

    Global Fussion is a leader in Audio Post Production for high-end clients, with whom we work locally and remotely. We are approved by both Audible and the SAG Aftra Union to conduct safe, socially distanced sessions during the COVID-19 Pandemic. Some of our post production services include: ADR and Voice Over, Mix to Picture: Stereo/5.1,Source Connect Pro, Scoring, and so on.

  • Podcasts/Audio Books

    In the past few years, Podcasts and Audio Books have exploded into some of the most popular and dynamic forms of new media. Whether youre telling immersive stories, holding engaging interviews or just having some laughs with your friends, Global Fussion has everything you need to create polished, professional Podcasts and Audio Books.
